# Constitution Placeholder

Replace this file with your organisation's actual constitution document.

The constitution should cover (at minimum):
- Name of the organisation
- Objects / purpose
- Membership (eligibility, fees, rights)
- Committee composition and election process
- Quorum requirements
- AGM requirements and notice period
- Amendment procedure
- Winding-up procedure

Once you have your constitution, store it here as `governance/constitution.md` (or as a PDF
linked from this file) and remove this placeholder.
